About Us

Our Story

Healing Resort founed in 2022, we are dedicated to bring visitors to a place that feels like home. It is a place where you can calm your mind in these noisy times, where you can take away the worries and stresses of everyday life by enjoying the hot springs of Karuizawa, the chirping of birds in the forest, and all the beauty of nature. In order to ensure that visitors are completely relaxed here, we use all natural ingredients and materials. The founder, Stephanie Liu, hopes that individuals who come here will have a wonderful memory with their family,friend, or even themselves.
